Dragon

Description

Dragon is a popular Filipino dish made from meat, soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kilo of pork
  • 1/2 cup of soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup of vinegar
  • 1 head of garlic

Steps

  1. Combine all the ingredients in a bowl
  2. Marinate the pork in the mixture
  3. Grill the pork until cooked
  4. Serve with garlic rice
