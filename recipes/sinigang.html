Sinigang

Description

Sinigang is a sour soup made from tamarind broth, various vegetables, and proteins.

  • 1/2 kilo of pork
  • 1/2 cup of soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup of vinegar
  • 1 head of garlic

Steps

  1. Boil the pork in water until tender.
  2. Add the tamarind broth and bring to a boil.
  3. Add the vegetables and proteins.
  4. Simmer until the vegetables are cooked.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
